The Valar

The Pantheon of Middle Earth

The Valar were the Powers of Arda who shaped and ruled the world. They lived on the Western continent of Aman.

Manwë

The King of the Winds, King of the Valar, and husband of Varda.

Ulmo

The Sea King, Lord of Water, responsible for the control over the oceans of Arda.

Melkor

(Morgoth) the First Dark Lord, the strongest Vala before he was banished from the group.

Varda

Mightiest of the Valier and Queen of the Valar, she was responsible for situating the stars in the heavens above Arda.
